Don’t forget to read our Deals page for some hot discounts on lodging, dining and activities. Single-night reservations for campgrounds, cabins and lodge rooms may be made for any night Sunday through Thursday. If you want to stay during the weekend, reservations require a minimum of two (2) nights—Friday and Saturday—with the exception of holiday weekends. A three-night minimum is required when a holiday causes a three-day weekend, i.e., Memorial Day weekend, July 4th weekend when applicable, Labor Day weekend, etc.
